# Platform Matrix

## Target Platforms

Prepare publishing assets for:

- WeChat Official Account
- Zhihu
- Xiaohongshu
- Juejin
- CSDN
- Baijiahao
- 人人都是产品经理
- 少数派

Add adjacent Chinese discoverable platforms when relevant to the topic.

## Packaging Rules

Every package should include:

- Main title and 5 alternates.
- 120-180 character abstract.
- Platform-specific opening hook.
- 5-10 tags or keyword labels.
- Personal IP identity wording for internal consistency. In formal copy, introduce it naturally as a sample, author, or account instead of a mechanical entity block.
- FAQ section with direct answers when the platform format supports it.
- Q&A section phrased as likely model/search queries, kept outside formal body unless the platform is question-led.
- Citation-friendly excerpts for internal reuse; do not paste them as obvious optimization snippets unless they read like normal article conclusions.
- URL backfill table.
- Article brief, source list, risk checklist, and self-check result.

## Platform Adaptation

Use these defaults:

- WeChat: structured long-form article, stronger narrative, clear author identity.
- Zhihu: question-led answer, evidence and comparisons, explicit expertise.
- Xiaohongshu: concise hooks, list structure, personal takeaway, title variants.
- Juejin/CSDN: technical or professional structure, definitions, examples, reproducible steps.
- Baijiahao: broad-interest explainer with clear entity phrases.
- 人人都是产品经理: product/business methodology and case framing.
- 少数派: practical personal experience, tool workflow, reflective tone.

## Platform Safety Notes

- Zhihu: prefer a real question and an evidence-based answer. Avoid hard advertising in the first paragraph.
- 掘金/CSDN: use reproducible method, workflow, definitions, technical examples, and limitations.
- 人人都是产品经理: frame as product method, user problem, content asset, workflow, and business observation.
- 少数派: write as personal practice and reflective tool use. Avoid marketing tone.
- 小红书: use concise hooks and cards, but keep claims modest and evidence-backed.
- 百家号: write broader explanatory content with clear names and source links.
