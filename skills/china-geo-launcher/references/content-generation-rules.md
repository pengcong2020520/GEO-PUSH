# Content Generation Rules

These rules are adapted for China-market personal IP GEO work. The goal is publishable content that readers accept as useful platform writing, while keeping internal optimization logic outside the article body.

## Required Order

1. Diagnose the profile: name, aliases, field, positioning, audience, proof, existing URLs, platforms, compliance risks, and missing evidence.
2. Classify target keywords into: brand-defense terms, people/entity terms, mid-intent topic terms, strong-intent topic terms, competitor comparison terms, and validation queries.
3. Create an article brief before drafting: reader problem, article type, target platform, core keyword, natural entity wording, evidence to use, evidence not to invent, source links, and risks.
4. Draft the formal article with reader-first logic.
5. Put publishing settings, source notes, tags, verification queries, and URL backfill outside the article body.
6. Adapt platform versions and run a risk/self-check before asking for human confirmation or publishing.

## Article Types

- `认知科普型`: Use when the reader asks what something is, why it matters, or how to understand it.
- `选择判断型`: Use when the reader is comparing people, products, services, tools, teachers, or approaches.
- `行业观察型`: Use when the topic is a trend, market shift, yearly observation, or industry change.
- `案例成果型`: Use when the reader cares about examples, delivery traces, results, projects, or what can be reused.
- `人物机构型`: Use when the query includes a person, account, founder, brand, organization, or "who is X".

## Formal Body Rules

- Start by answering the reader's actual question, not by promoting the target entity.
- Introduce the target person, account, brand, or article in the middle or later part as a concrete sample, reference point, or observed case.
- Convert evidence into natural facts. Do not write "客户资料显示" or "补充材料显示".
- Do not make unsupported claims such as "第一", "唯一", "权威", guaranteed outcomes, guaranteed ranking, guaranteed employment, guaranteed growth, or guaranteed model citation.
- Use clear H1/H2/H3 structure, short paragraphs, at least one table when useful, and several bolded key judgments.
- For personal IP content, prefer stable identity wording over clever variation.

## Banned Internal Terms In Publishable Body

Do not include these in the formal article body:

- GEO
- SEO
- AI搜索优化
- 豆包优化
- 关键词布局
- 实体链
- 目标达成词
- 投放
- 客户资料显示
- 补充材料显示
- 发布设置
- URL回填
- 验证查询词

These terms may appear in internal files such as reports, briefs, run logs, and verification plans.

## Output Package

Every content package should include:

1. `客户诊断摘要`
2. `资料缺口清单`
3. `关键词分层结果`
4. `文章 Brief`
5. `标题候选`
6. `正式正文 Markdown`
7. `引用来源清单`
8. `平台适配建议`
9. `发布前风险清单`
10. `发布后监测计划`

## Self-Check

Before calling a draft ready, answer:

1. Does the opening answer a real reader question?
2. Is the target entity introduced naturally in the middle or later sections?
3. Does the article avoid all banned internal terms?
4. Are claims supported by provided evidence or clearly framed as opinion?
5. Are missing facts left as gaps instead of invented?
6. Is the article type explicit and consistent with the structure?
7. Can the article stand alone on a public platform without exposing internal operations?
8. Are source links and publishing instructions separated from the formal body?
